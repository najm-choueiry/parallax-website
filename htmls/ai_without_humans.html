Now What can AI not do without humans?

Although AI have created fascinating intelligent machines that can replace humans, but they are miles away from matching the human brain’s potential.

AI can’t make logical judgements like us although they are trained to imitate human behavior.

Moreover, AI machines lack common sense; they can’t understand the “cause and effect” notion. Whereas humans have the ability to comprehend and apply what they learned using logical, reasoned, and emotional approach.
